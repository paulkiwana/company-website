Who We Are

Background

Jose River Coatings is a fast-growing private limited company founded in 2015 to meet the demands of the world market for paint solutions. We started as a manufacturer of Emulsions, then expanded into Decorative and Texture paints. Our unique products have given us a leading position in the world of paints.

Vision

To be the leading producer and supplier of high-quality and environmentally friendly paints and related products in the African Continent.

Goals

  • To offer the best option for our clients to maximize their satisfaction in designing and painting.
  • To provide products and services to our customers at short notice and best rates.
  • To diversify our products and services to cater to clients' needs without compromising quality.
