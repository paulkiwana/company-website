Who We Are

Background

Jose River Coatings is a fast-growing private limited company founded in 2015 to meet the demands of the world market for paint solutions. We started as a manufacturer of Emulsions, then expanded into Decorative and Texture paints. Our unique products have given us a leading position in the world of paints.

Vision

To be the leading producer and supplier of high-quality and environmentally friendly paints and related products in the African Continent.

Goals

  • To offer the best option for our clients to maximize their satisfaction in designing and painting.
  • To provide products and services to our customers at short notice and best rates.
  • To diversify our products and services to cater to clients' needs without compromising quality.

Primer

Primer paint is special coating used on surfaces before painting. It is designed improve adhesion of paint to the surface, Increase paints durability, offer additional protection for the material being painted, acts as a sealer for porous surfaces and act as a stain blocker for walls

Texture Emprofile(Paste)

A very hard washable internal and external plasticized coating based on acrylic resins, using fine crushed aggregates and inorganic color fast pigments creating a texture with varying degrees of vertical stiations. Together with our acrylic glaze finish, the texture will self maintain as dirt and residues can be washed away easily

Ritmix(Rough and tough)

An acrylic resin based texture, which is engineered as a rough finish with excellent resistant qualities. Because the pigments fully dispersed within the texture, this product maintains color and finish for many years. Together with our acrylic glaze finish, the texture will self maintain as dirt and residues can be washed away easily

Glaze

Glaze paint is a special kind of paint that is used to add texture and depth to surface. It is a semi-transparent layer applied over a complementary solid color or basecoat.It is used to create rich, iridescent colors and used for decorative walls, art and furniture

Stone Finish

An extremely superior coating using natural quartzite in sizes ranging from 1mm to 3mm embedded in an enhanced acrylic blinder which is extremely hard and durable producing an everlasting stone look

Emcourse

A heavy body heavy textured coating for internal and external use. It exhibits excellent obliteration properties and flexibility making it ideal to cover hairline cracks and poor plasterwork. Together with our acrylic glaze finish, the texture will self maintain as dirt and residues can be washed away easily
