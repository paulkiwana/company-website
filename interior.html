Undercoat

This can be used on interior and exterior concrete, plaster, wood, metal. It prepares the surface an ideally smooth foundation for the finishing coats. it can be applied by brush, rollers or spray gun.

Matt Emulsion

It's easy to apply and produces a beautiful matt finish, which hides all surface impactions. Matt Emulsion can be used on interior and exterior surfaces like; conccrete, plaster, bricks, and even previously painted walls. It can also be applied brush, rollers, and spray.

Italian Stucco

The marble like finish is magnificent on feature walls,columns,staircase,reception arears and is suitable for indoors application.

Vinyl Silk

Vinyl Silk is a type of paint that is known for it's durable, shiny finish and is used for interior design projects.
